AXOS BANK - PERSONAL LOAN POLICY
Last Updated: January 2024

1. LOAN LIMITS
Personal loans are available from $1,000 to $50,000.
Business loans are available from $10,000 to $500,000.
Home equity loans are available up to 80% of property value.

2. WIRE TRANSFERS
Domestic wire transfers: maximum $25,000 per day for personal accounts.
Business accounts: maximum $100,000 per day.
International wire transfers require 2 business days processing time.
All wire transfers over $10,000 are reported to FinCEN per BSA requirements.

3. OVERDRAFT POLICY
Overdraft protection is available for checking accounts in good standing.
Maximum overdraft limit is $500 for personal accounts.
Overdraft fee is $35 per transaction.
Accounts overdrawn for more than 30 days will be referred to collections.

4. ACCOUNT REQUIREMENTS
Minimum opening deposit for checking: $25.
Minimum opening deposit for savings: $100.
Business accounts require EIN and two forms of identification.

5. LOAN APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
Government issued photo ID.
Last 2 years tax returns.
Last 3 months bank statements.
Proof of employment or income.
Minimum credit score of 650 required.
